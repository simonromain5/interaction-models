Common calulations¶

common_calc.find_couples(neighbors, t)¶

This function finds all the couples at time t. The couples are all particles i and j that are neighbors at a particular time.

Parameters:
  • neighbors (np.array of lists) – Array of all the neighbors of particle i
  • t (float or int) – time of the iteration. It is equal to step * dt.
common_calc.projection(vector, velocity_i, velocity_j)¶

This function returns True if velocity_i and velocity_j face opposite directions considering the vector that links the center of particle i and particle j. :param vector: vector that links the center of particle i and particle j. :type vector: np.array :param velocity_i: vector of the velocity of particle i :type velocity_i: np.array :param velocity_j: vector of the velocity of particle j :type velocity_j: np.array :return: True if the two velocity vectors face opposite directions :rtype: bool
